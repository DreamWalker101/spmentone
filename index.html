About St Patrick's

Cultivating Faith-Centered Learning and Community Engagement

St. Patrick’s, situated in the Archdiocese of Melbourne, offers a transformative educational experience rooted in Catholic values. Our nurturing environment encourages students to grow spiritually, academically, and personally. Join our inclusive community where faith, action, and relationships flourish.
