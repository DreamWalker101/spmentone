ST PATRICK’S - ENROLMENT INFORMATION

Welcome to St Patrick’s Catholic Primary School. We hope that we can begin a positive partnership with you and your child focused on the development of your child.

Enrolments for Prep 2027 are now closed. Enrolments for Prep 2028 will be open from late 2026. Historically we have a strong demand for places, therefore parents are encouraged to apply early in the 2027 school year.
Letters of offer for Prep 2028 will be advised at the end of April 2027. The unsuccessful applications will stay on the waitlist and may receive second or third round offers later in the year.
Please note, applications for Prep are accepted the year before a child is due to start school.

If you would like more information about St Patrick’s, please send an email to principal@spmentone.catholic.edu.au informing the school of your name and a contact number and we will be happy to answer any questions you may have. School Tours can be made through appointment.

ENROLMENT INFORMATON

Age

• Children must turn 5 years of age before the 30th April in the year they are commencing school.

Enrolment Area

• Our school has a specific enrolment area, which encompasses the area of Mentone and the surrounding suburbs. Any enrolments from outside this area must be dealt with on an individual basis.

Enrolment Procedure

• An initial enrolment is obtained at the Open Day or from our website and enrolments are then open until April 28th. Families will need to provide a copy of your child’s Birth, Baptism and Immunisation documents which will be retained by the school. Once enrolments close, the principal and deputy principal will have an interview with each family. At this appointment the school will:

• meet the parent/s and child

• explain the current fee payment system

• answer any queries

• give insight into our school culture

In April/May a letter confirming your child’s enrolment will be sent to you. You will be asked to send back a reply slip indicating your acceptance of this Prep placement for the coming year.

2027 Prep placement acceptances closing date.

Signed acceptance form and enrolment deposit payment*.

*To accept the enrolment offer, a non-refundable enrolment deposit of $500 is payable. This amount is credited to your 2027 school account on commencement.

We will also notify you of the dates for these 2 events namely:

I. Prep Information Night is held in early November. Parent/s attend this night without children. We will introduce you to your child’s Prep teacher. The Prep teacher will explain the routines of the Prep day and discuss the Prep curriculum.

II. Orientation Sessions are held in November. Your child will attend two orientation sessions. They will go to a classroom to complete orientation activities. In the meantime, parents will enjoy a cup of tea in the hall.

This means that you will be all set for the big day next year by December of this year!

Enrolment Time

• Usually around February/March, kindergartens and schools will advertise that it is now time to enrol your child at school. Our enrolments commence with our Open Days in February/March and will close on April 24th.

ACCEPTANCE OF A NON-CATHOLIC STUDENT AT OUR PARISH SCHOOL DOES NOT GUARANTEE ENROLMENT AT A CATHOLIC SECONDARY COLLEGE.

Please note the following:

  1. To be eligible for enrolment your child must turn five years old by 30th April, in the year they are commencing school.
  2. A copy of your child’s Baptism Certificate must be provided. This should be attached to your Enrolment Form.
  3. Non-Catholic families lodging an enrolment will need to include a personal letter clearly stating the reasons they are seeking enrolment in a Catholic school.
  4. At the time of lodging an enrolment application all new families will be required to pay a non-refundable enrolment fee of $50.00 per family for administrative purposes.
  5. An immunisation certificate must be provided for all children, prior to their commencement at school, this can be obtained from the Medicare Office.
  6. Prep Enrolment Applications will close on Friday 24th April, 2026.
  7. Individual interviews will be conducted for all new enrolment applicants. Times will be arranged by an online booking system.
  8. An Information Night for parents will be held in Term 4.
  9. School Orientation Program will be held in Term 4 for all new prep students.
